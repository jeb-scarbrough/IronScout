## What to know about .45 ACP FMJ

The 230gr FMJ is the universal .45 ACP range load. Virtually all brands use this weight. The round is naturally subsonic (~830 fps), making it suppressor-friendly even without special loads. Price spreads between brands are tight — buy whatever's cheapest and in stock.
