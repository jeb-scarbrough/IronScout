## What to know

.223 Remington and 5.56 NATO FMJ are functionally interchangeable in most modern rifles. .223-labeled ammo is loaded to SAAMI spec (slightly lower pressure than 5.56 NATO). Wolf Gold .223 is consistently the cheapest brass-case option. See also [5.56 FMJ](/caliber/556-nato/fmj) for more options.
