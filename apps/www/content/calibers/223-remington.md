## How .223 Remington prices compare

The .223 Remington is dimensionally identical to 5.56 NATO but loaded to lower SAAMI pressure. In practice, most shooters use them interchangeably in 5.56 or .223 Wylde chambers. Prices are very similar to 5.56 NATO — FMJ range ammo runs $0.28–0.40 per round. Wolf Gold .223 is consistently one of the cheapest brass-cased options available. The .223 label is more common on precision and varmint loads, while 5.56 NATO designates military-spec ball ammunition.
