## ADR Template

Use this exact template for all ADRs.

```markdown
# ADR-XXX: <Short Decision Title>

## Status
Accepted | Proposed | Superseded

## Context
Describe the problem or decision to be made.
Include relevant constraints and background.

## Decision
State the decision clearly and unambiguously.

## Alternatives Considered
List meaningful alternatives and why they were not chosen.

## Consequences
Describe the impact of this decision:
- Technical
- Operational
- Product or trust-related

## Notes
Optional clarifications or follow-ups.
